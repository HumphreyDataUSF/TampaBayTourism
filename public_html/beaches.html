Fort De Soto's North Beach

Discover the beauty of Fort De Soto's North Beach. Walk out on the sandbar, collect shells, and enjoy the Caribbean feel.

Best times to visit:

  • Early spring for calm waters and good UV protection.
  • Avoid midday for intense sun exposure.

Tips:

  • The sandbar shifts frequently, so make sure to check tide times for the best experience.
  • There are plenty of shells to collect, but it gets busy during peak seasons, so plan accordingly.

Clearwater Beach

Known for its lively atmosphere and stunning sunsets, Clearwater Beach is a must-visit, but beware of its touristy crowds.

Best times to visit:

  • Visit late in the afternoon for a peaceful sunset.
  • Avoid peak seasons for a less crowded experience.

Tips:

  • Parking can be tough, so either arrive early or use public transport or rideshare services.
  • Don't miss the nightly sunset celebrations; though it gets crowded, it's a fun spectacle!
