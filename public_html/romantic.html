Dining at Bern's Steak House

Indulge in a fine dining experience at Bern's Steak House, one of Tampa's most celebrated restaurants. Known for its gourmet cuisine and intimate ambiance, it's perfect for a special night out.

Best Times to Visit:

  • Weekdays for a quieter experience, especially in the evening.
  • Reservations are highly recommended, particularly on weekends.

Tips:

  • Try the iconic Chateaubriand steak, a favorite among regulars.
  • Visit the unique dessert room for a one-of-a-kind experience.
  • Parking is limited - valet service or nearby lots are convenient options.

Private Picnic at Fort De Soto

Plan a romantic picnic at Fort De Soto, one of Tampa Bay's most scenic and serene spots. Enjoy the peaceful North Beach, where you can relax in nature's tranquility.

Best Times to Visit:

  • Spring and early fall for pleasant weather and fewer crowds.
  • Weekdays to avoid the busier weekend rush.

Tips:

  • Bring a blanket, picnic essentials, and outdoor games for a relaxing day.
  • Check the tide schedule for the best sandbar access during low tide.

Sunset Cruise in the Gulf of Mexico

Set sail on a romantic sunset cruise along the Gulf of Mexico. With breathtaking views of the water and sky, it's an unforgettable way to end the day.

Best Times to Visit:

  • Evenings, typically around 6-7 PM, for the most stunning sunset views.
  • Spring and fall, when the weather is ideal for cruising.

Tips:

  • Bring a light jacket - it can get chilly on the water after sunset.
  • Most cruises include snacks and drinks, so confirm what's offered when booking.
