ZooTampa

Experience over 1,000 animals and interactive exhibits suitable for children of all ages.

Best times to visit:

  • Weekdays for fewer crowds.
  • Morning visits are great to see the animals more active.

Tips:

  • Parking can fill up fast during peak hours, so arrive early or use a rideshare service.
  • Bring sunscreen, as there’s plenty of outdoor walking.

Busch Gardens

Perfect for families looking for adventure with thrilling rides and animal encounters.

Best times to visit:

  • Weekdays are less crowded, especially in the off-season.
  • Arrive early to enjoy the popular rides before the lines get long.

Tips:

  • Don’t miss the Serengeti Safari, a highlight for animal lovers!
  • Wear comfortable shoes, as there’s a lot of walking involved.

Lowry Park Zoo

Another great zoo with family-friendly exhibits and a variety of interactive experiences.

Best times to visit:

  • Morning visits are ideal for cooler temperatures and more active animals.
  • Weekdays are best for avoiding weekend crowds.

Tips:

  • Check for special events or activities, like animal encounters, before you go.
  • Bring a picnic and enjoy a meal in one of their designated picnic areas.
